Description

A simple and delicous traditional recipe for bolognese sauce

Ingredients

  • 600g Minced Beef
  • 2-3 medium/big Carrots
  • 3-4 Gloves Garlic
  • 1 Fennel
  • 2-3 Celery
  • 3-5 Shallots
  • 200ml Beef broth
  • White Wine
  • 400g Strained Tomatoes
  • Salt, Pepper and dry Italian Herbs
